name: art-ideation
description: A brainstorming skill tailored specifically for an ART theme hackathon (visual, stage, audio). Use this when generating project ideas or fleshing out concepts.

When activated:
1. Ask the user about the mediums they want to combine (e.g., Code + Audio, Generative Art + Web, Interactive Stage Lighting).
2. Push the user to focus on a high "WOW Factor" – ideas that immediately look or sound impressive within the first 10 seconds of interaction.
3. Suggest 3 bold, wildly different ideas integrating modern tech (like WebGL, Web Audio API, or AI image/sound generation).
4. Iterate on the chosen idea until the concept is solid, novel, and achievable in 24 hours.
